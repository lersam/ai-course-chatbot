**Question:** In the story "Tom Tit Tot," what is the secret name the little black impet sings while spinning?
**Answer:** The name is "Tom Tit Tot." The king hears the impet singing, "Nimmy nimmy not / My name’s Tom Tit Tot" while he is out hunting in the woods.

**Question:** In "The Rose-Tree," what does the little boy do with his sister’s remains after the wicked stepmother kills her?

**Question:** Why did the third little pig build his house out of bricks, and how did that choice change his fate?
**Answer:** The third little pig built his house out of bricks because he met a man with a load of bricks and asked for them to build a house with. This choice changed his fate because the wolf was unable to blow his house down, unlike the other two pigs.

**Question:** How does the little pig finally defeat the wolf in "The Story of the Three Bears" (specifically the version involving the wolf)?
**Answer:** When the wolf tries to come down the chimney, the little pig hangs a pot full of water over a blazing fire; the wolf falls in, and the pig boils him and eats him for supper.
